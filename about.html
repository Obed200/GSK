Our History

Founded in 2001, GS Kabuga has been dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders. Over the years, we have grown from a small community school to a recognized institution in Kabuga City, known for academic excellence and holistic education.

Our Mission

To provide a conducive learning environment that empowers students to excel academically, socially, and morally, preparing them to contribute meaningfully to society.
