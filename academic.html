Overview of Offered Programs

At G.S SAINT ANTOINE, we offer programs to meet the needs of students at every level:

  • Nursery: Early childhood education focused on foundational skills.
  • Primary: Comprehensive education for foundational learning.
  • Ordinary Level (O Level): Core subjects aimed at academic growth.
  • A Level Programs:
    • MCE: Mathematics, Chemistry, Economics.
    • HEGL: History, Economics, Geography, Literature.
    • LFKK: Literature, French, Kiswahili, Kinyarwanda.

Class Schedule and Subjects

Our schedule ensures a balanced learning experience:

  • Morning: 7:30 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Lunch Break: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
  • Afternoon: 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Subjects Offered: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Languages, History, Geography, and more.

Academic Calendar

Our academic year is divided into three terms:

  • Term 1: January - April
  • Term 2: May - August
  • Term 3: September - December
